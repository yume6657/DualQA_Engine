The 2026 Internship Program at DualQA Tech Center offers a special "Innovation Bonus".
Interns who complete their first Django project within 30 days are eligible for a
one-time scholarship of 5000 CNY. Additionally, every Friday afternoon is designated
as "Code & Coffee Time," where interns can claim a free Caramel Macchiato at the
university's north gate cafeteria by showing their digital ID.
